Welcome to CiteCount

Word counting solution for students and researchers, built by an IB student.

What is CiteCount?

CiteCount is a tool to count words without citations from your text. Do you have an assignment which requires you to include a word count without citations? CiteCount is the tool for you.

With CiteCount, you track and count number of citations you have used, words you have used without citations, and characters you have used without citations.

We provide an advanced algorithms to ensure you get the most accurate citation counts - all done within your browser.

AutoSave is integrated to the text editor of CiteCount, making sure you won't lose any changes while editing, even if you leave the site and come back later.

Word counts are real time updated, so you can see the results as you type.

Most importantly, this web application is ad free and open source. You can use it for free, without any restrictions. View the source on GitHub.
